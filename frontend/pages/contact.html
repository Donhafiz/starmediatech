Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 business hours during our working hours. For urgent matters, please call our support line for immediate assistance.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer free 30-minute initial consultations to discuss your project requirements and determine how we can help you achieve your goals.

What are your payment terms?

We require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer various support packages including monthly maintenance, hosting, and technical support to ensure your project continues to perform optimally.
