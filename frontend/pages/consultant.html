Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our consultation services

What happens during a consultation session?

During your consultation, you'll have a one-on-one video call with your chosen expert. You'll discuss your project goals, challenges, and requirements. The consultant will provide personalized advice, answer your questions, and help you create a clear action plan for your project.

Can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled session without any charges. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a 50% fee. You can manage your bookings through your account dashboard.

What if I need more time than scheduled?

If you need additional time, you can discuss extending the session with your consultant during the call. Additional time is billed in 15-minute increments at a prorated rate based on the consultant's hourly fee.

Do you offer ongoing consultation packages?

Yes, we offer monthly retainer packages for clients who need ongoing support. These packages include regular check-ins, priority support, and discounted rates. Contact our sales team to learn more about our retainer options.
